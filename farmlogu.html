Double-Entry Accounting

Real general ledger. Chart of accounts, journal entries, balance sheet, income statement. CORE + vertical COA pattern.

EPCIS 2.0 Traceability

GS1 standard event-based tracing. GTIN, GLN, SSCC identifiers. Forward & backward chain. FSMA 204 + EU 178/2002 compliant.

Hash-Chain Audit Trail

SHA-256 linked events. Tamper-evident. Every action recorded with IP, user, timestamp. Immutable for regulatory compliance.
